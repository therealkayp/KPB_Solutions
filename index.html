Our Story

I am Kay, a passionate full-stack developer with a unique entrepreneurial spirit. Originally from Jamaica, I migrated to the U.S., where I turned my tech skills and creativity into thriving businesses.

As the founder of Ashabi Hair Extensions, Ashabi Apparel, and a successful Amazon seller, I seamlessly blend IT expertise with my love for fashion and storytelling. With over a decade of experience in web development, I specialize in building modern websites and web applications.

Driven by an entrepreneurial mindset, I founded Ashabi Apparel, where I design trendy and functional fitness wear. My technical skills enable me to create a seamless online shopping experience, merging fashion and technology.

My journey reflects the perfect blend of technical prowess and entrepreneurial vision—transforming ideas into reality across multiple industries.
